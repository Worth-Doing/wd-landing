GridForge

Next-Generation Spreadsheet Engine

A native macOS spreadsheet with a real formula engine, XLSX import/export, 50+ built-in functions, multi-sheet tabs, inspector panel, and full dark mode support. 64 passing tests, 7,633 lines of Swift, 2.4 MB DMG.

● Capabilities

Features

ƒ

50+ Formula Functions

SUM, AVERAGE, IF, VLOOKUP, COUNTIF, CONCATENATE, DATE functions, and more. Real formula parser with cell references and ranges.

📄

XLSX Import & Export

Open and save Excel .xlsx files natively. CSV export also supported. Seamless compatibility with existing spreadsheet workflows.

📑

Multi-Sheet Tabs

Create, rename, and switch between multiple sheets. Tab bar at the bottom like traditional spreadsheets.

🔎

Inspector Panel

Side panel with Cell Info, Font & Text formatting, Colors, and Sheet Info sections. Full cell formatting controls.

📈

Live Statistics

Select a range and see Sum, Average, Count instantly in the status bar. Formula bar shows cell content and address.

↺

Undo/Redo & Dark Mode

Full command-pattern undo/redo. Complete dark mode support. 64 automated tests covering models, formulas, and XLSX handling.

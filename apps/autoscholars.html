AutoScholar

Autonomous Scientific Research Engine

The first fully standalone macOS app that generates publication-grade academic research papers autonomously. No server — runs entirely on your Mac using Claude, local Python analysis, and local LaTeX compilation. 7 specialized AI agents orchestrate a 7-phase research pipeline from gap analysis to peer review.

Features

📚

7 Specialized Agents

Euler (writer), Gauss (gap analysis), Turing (data collection), Newton (data prep), Fisher (analysis), Governor (supervisor), Popper (peer review).

📊

4,018 Research Prompts

24 domains across finance, econometrics, ML, and more. 24 methodological approaches from Panel Regression to Bayesian MCMC.

🌐

8+ Data Providers

FMP, FRED, EODHD, World Bank, IMF, OECD, Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, arXiv, CrossRef, Tavily, Firecrawl, Exa, Polymarket.

📄

Local LaTeX Compilation

pdflatex multi-pass compilation with iterative error repair. Native PDFKit viewer with zoom, export, and print.

🔥

Local Python Analysis

NumPy, Pandas, SciPy, Statsmodels, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, XGBoost, LightGBM — figures and econometrics without leaving your Mac.

⚙

Governor System

Rule-based quality control with zero LLM calls. 7 profiles, retry budgets, confidence tracking, and publication verdicts.
